My Top 3 Movies (September 2023)

Fantastic Mr. Fox (2009)

dir: Wes Anderson

Starring: George Clooney, Meryl Streep, Jason Schwartzman, Bill Murray

The Fantastic Mr. Fox, bored with his current life, plans a heist against the three local farmers.
The farmers, tired of sharing their chickens with the sly fox, seek revenge against him and his family.


Dunkirk (2017)

dir: Christopher Nolan

Starring: Fionn Whitehead, Tom Hardy, Cillian Murphy, Mark Rylance

The story of the miraculous evacuation of Allied soldiers from Belgium, Britain, Canada and France,
who were cut off and surrounded by the German army from the beaches and harbour of Dunkirk between May 26th and June 4th 1940 during World War II.


The Shawshank Redemption (1994)

dir: Frank Darabont

Starring: Tim Robins, Morgan Freeman, Bob Gunton, William Sadler

Framed in the 1940s for the double murder of his wife and her lover, upstanding banker Andy Dufresne begins a new life at the Shawshank prison, where he puts his accounting skills to work for an amoral warden.
During his long stretch in prison, Dufresne comes to be admired by the other inmates – including an older prisoner named Red – for his integrity and unquenchable sense of hope.
